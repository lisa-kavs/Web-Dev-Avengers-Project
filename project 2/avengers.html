The Avengers

When Thors evil brother Loki invaded Earth with his army (Chitauri), the avengers we know today came together and formed S.H.I.E.L.D. S.H.I.E.L.D stands for Strategic Homeland Intervention, Enforcement, and Logistics Division.Nick Fury is the man who formed S.H.I.E.L.D. The first Avengers Movie came out in 2012 and was named "The Avengers". The first Avenger Movie made 370.6 million dollars.
